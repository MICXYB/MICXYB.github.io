I lead the Data Mining Group of Mobile Intelligent Computing Lab (MIC Lab). My research interests span information retrieval, spatio-temporal data mining, and urban computing. I have over 40 publications appeared in several top conferences such as INFOCOM, ICDE, and journals including TKDE, TMC, and TNNLS. Moreover, I have served as (senior) PC member for conferences including WWW, KDD, IJCAI, AAAI, etc.
